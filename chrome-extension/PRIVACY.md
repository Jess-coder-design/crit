# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection

The CR!T Orange Blur extension does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data. 

## What We Track

- **Local Storage Only**: User preferences (such as whether the blur effect is enabled) are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's `storage.local` API. This data never leaves your device.
- **No External Communication**: The extension does not send any information to external servers or third parties.
- **No Analytics**: We do not use any analytics or tracking services.

## Permissions Explanation

- **scripting**: Used to inject the blur effect and keyword highlighting into webpages you visit
- **activeTab**: Used to access the current webpage for applying effects
- **storage**: Used to remember your preference settings locally

## Contact

For privacy concerns, please reach out to the extension developer.
